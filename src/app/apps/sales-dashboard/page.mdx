<Section id="about">
  ## About

  This project was built to help a hardware business understand its sales performance across multiple regions in India. The business had raw transaction data spanning FY 2017–2020 across 15+ cities and 38 stores but lacked a visual tool to make sense of it.

  The goal was to transform this raw data into a dynamic, interactive dashboard that decision-makers could actually use — no data science background required.
</Section>

<Border className="my-8" />

<Section id="challenge">
  ## Problem Statement

  The AtliQ hardware company needed answers to questions like:

  1. Which cities and stores are generating the most revenue?
  2. How are sales trending year-over-year from FY 2017 to FY 2020?
  3. Which product categories have the best profit margins?
  4. Where are the underperforming stores pulling down overall numbers?

  The raw data existed in a MySQL database but there was no visual layer on top of it for business stakeholders to explore.
</Section>

<Border className="my-8" />

<Section id="solution">

## Solution

<AppSolution href={appData.url}>

**Data Pipeline:** Connected Power BI directly to a MySQL database, wrote SQL queries to clean and join the relevant tables — transactions, customers, markets, products, and date dimensions.

**ETL in Power Query:** Filtered invalid records (negative sales values, test transactions), normalized currency across regions, and removed duplicate entries to ensure data quality.

**Dashboard Design:** Built a multi-page Power BI report with:

<ul role="list" className="mt-6 space-y-8">
  <li>
    <span>
      <strong className="font-semibold text-blue-100">Revenue Trend View:</strong> Line chart showing monthly and yearly revenue from FY 2017–2020, with slicers to filter by region and product category.
    </span>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>
      <strong className="font-semibold text-blue-100">KPI Cards:</strong> Summary tiles for Total Revenue, Total Sales Quantity, and Profit Margin % — updating dynamically with every filter selection.
    </span>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>
      <strong className="font-semibold text-blue-100">City & Store Breakdown:</strong> Bar charts ranking all 15+ cities and 38 stores by revenue contribution, making it easy to spot top performers and laggards instantly.
    </span>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>
      <strong className="font-semibold text-blue-100">Profit Margin Analysis:</strong> Scatter plot mapping sales quantity vs. profit margin per product, exposing high-volume but low-margin items.
    </span>
  </li>
</ul>

**Outcome:** The final dashboard gave business stakeholders a single source of truth for sales performance, replacing weekly manual Excel reports with a live, self-service analytics tool.

</AppSolution>
